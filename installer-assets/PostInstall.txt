Installation complete!

To begin I recommend you by reading the Sample_PROBLEM_LIST.txt file.

Then rename Sample_PROBLEM_LIST.txt to just PROBLEM_LIST.txt, then run the ChessNavigator executable!

PROBLEM_LIST.txt contains your problems.
custom_pieces.yml contains any fairy pieces you define.
config.json contains any cosmetic changes to the interface.
fairy_piece_blocks.json (Advanced) to customize the side panel of fairy pieces.
